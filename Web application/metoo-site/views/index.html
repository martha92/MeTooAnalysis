About

We have often observed that disrespectful behavior towards women is due to lack of awareness or due to certain disbeliefs in the society.
Our Goal: Suggest Targeted Approach to spread awareness and help in the society.

Resolution

With more than 500,000 retweets and 12 million posts in Facebook within first 24 hours, #MeToo soon became a voice strong enough to bring down 201 Powerful Men according to The New York Times. Through this project we intend to analyze the virality of data from Data Science perspective and add value to the social cause behind this movement.

Our analysis attempts to:

  • Collectively analyze multiple social media platforms and identify real accounts.
  • Cluster people based on the common topics that they are discussing.
  • find the main influencers of each of these clusters.
  • Analyze the sentiments of these clusters.

Our Product would help the government or the NGOs easily identify group of people with similar beliefs and rapidly spread awareness or broadcast legal information/helpline numbers to the intended clusters through the influencers.

Key Features: An interactive web App depicting

  • Tend in sentiments over time.
  • Topic distribution.
  • Statistical insights.

Data Science Pipeline

Data Collection & Storge

Data Source: Instagram, Twitter
Extraction: Scrape posts, Get tweets API client
Total Volume: 1.4 GB

Data Cleaning & EDA

Data Cleaning: Remove stopwords & unwanted characters, Tag parts of speech, Lemmenting.
Library: NLTK, TextBlob
EDA: get statistical insights

Fake Account Detection

Goal: Classify fake vs real accounts.
Methods: Random Forest + Adaboost, NeuralNetworks Text Mining.

Topic Modelling

Goal: Get the optimal number of topics & find the dominant topics of each tweet.
Model: LDA, MalletLDA.
Library: PyLDAVis Gensim, NLTK.

Network Clusters

Goal:Design community clusters of Retweet and Topic network and find primary influencers.
Model: LPA, Louvain Modularity, PageRank.
Library:SparkFrame.

Sentiment Analysis

Goal:Calculate sentiments of each topic cluster based on hashtags network (scores from Loopy Belief algorithm) and tweets (TextBlob score).
Model:Naive Bayes Classifier.

Visualization

Goal: Depict trend of sentiments and distribution of topics over time. Compare involvement of users in MeToo as compared to their popularity.
